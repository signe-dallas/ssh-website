What Are Toolkits?

Practical, research-backed resources that help institutions turn student success strategy into action.

Toolkit Overview

The Student Success Toolkits are comprehensive, implementation-focused resources designed to help colleges and universities adopt proven student success strategies. Rather than offering theory alone, each toolkit brings together the tools, guidance, and examples institutions need to move from planning to execution.

Each toolkit is designed to support institutions through every phase of change and implementation.

Image / Video placeholder

What's Inside a Toolkits?

  • Implementation frameworks and roadmaps to guide planning and execution
  • Customizable templates and tools that teams can adapt to their local context
  • Case studies and success stories from peer institutions
  • Measurement and evaluation resources to track progress and outcomes

How Institutions Use Toolkits?

  • Assess. Understand your institution's current state, identify gaps, and establish baseline metrics for success.
  • Plan. Develop a strategic roadmap, engage stakeholders, and build your implementation team and timeline.
  • Act. Execute your plan using toolkit resources, templates, and best practices from peer institutions.
  • Measure. Track outcomes, evaluate impact, and refine your approach based on data and feedback.

What Outcomes Can Institutions Expect?

  • Improved student outcomes and retention
  • Better alignment between strategy, budget, and operations
  • More consistent, data-driven decision-making
  • Sustainable and scalable student success efforts
