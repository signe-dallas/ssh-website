What Are Toolkits?

Comprehensive, research-backed resources designed to help your institution implement proven student success strategies.

Comprehensive Resources for Student Success

Toolkits are comprehensive, research-backed resources designed to help your institution implement proven student success strategies. Each toolkit combines frameworks, templates, implementation guides, and best practices to support your team through every phase of change.

What You Get

  • Implementation frameworks and roadmaps
  • Customizable templates and tools
  • Case studies and success stories
  • Measurement and evaluation resources

Who Uses Them

  • Finance and budget teams
  • Student affairs professionals
  • Academic leaders and faculty
  • Institutional researchers

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved student outcomes and retention
  • Better resource allocation
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Sustainable institutional change

How Institutions Use Toolkits

Assess

Understand your institution's current state, identify gaps, and establish baseline metrics for success.

Plan

Develop a strategic roadmap, engage stakeholders, and build your implementation team and timeline.

Act

Execute your plan using toolkit resources, templates, and best practices from peer institutions.

Measure

Track outcomes, evaluate impact, and refine your approach based on data and feedback.
