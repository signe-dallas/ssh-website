Data to Inform Decisions

Data and Systems Governance Guide

The role of Chief Business Officers (CBOs) frequently involves spearheading the creation of business value. CBOs offer a unique opportunity to serve as a driving force for decisions guided by data. Because they routinely engage with crucial data across the organization, CBOs are increasingly transforming this data into practical, forward-thinking insights that surpass conventional reporting duties. This toolkit provides CBOs with a constructed framework ? visually represented as a journey map - to support colleges and universities on a path to responsible data infrastructure, management, and stewardship while advancing the use of analytics to support the institution's mission, vision, and values.

Browse All Toolkits

Value Proposition

Aids in developing policies and procedures to facilitate the collection, management, validation, and use of data as an institutional asset.

Supports the creation of an institution-wide culture that values collaboration to support data-informed decisions.

Implementation Options

Choose the level of adoption that fits your institution's capacity and goals. Every toolkit offers flexible implementation paths from quick wins to comprehensive integration.

Low Lift? Quick Start

Utilize the Knowledge phase to cultivate a foundational understanding of data governance principles without extensive organizational restructuring.

Mid Lift? Deeper Integration

Establish the Foundation phase to initiate stakeholder engagement and expertise development in data management.

High Lift? Full Implementation

Advance through the Capacity and Adoption phases to institutionalize robust data management and governance practices.
