Public 2-year Improvement Project
Toolkit Used: Budget Equity Blueprint

Allocating Resources to Enhance Equitable Student Outcomes

Problem

Budget allocation processes did not consistently use disaggregated student data, limiting the institution’s ability to target resources toward equity gaps and student success priorities.

Outcomes

  • Shifted resources to prioritize case management advising for students needing the most support
  • Allocated an additional $2M to targeted advising strategies
  • Strengthened data-informed budgeting focused on closing equity gaps
  • Established a repeatable process for equitable resource allocation

Public 4-year Improvement Project
Toolkit Used: Budget Process Assessment

Improving Budget Office Practices and Budget Processes

Problem

Enrollment declines created a structural budget deficit, while inconsistent communication and limited budget literacy made it difficult for campus stakeholders to navigate budgeting processes.

Outcomes

  • Identified campus-wide gaps in awareness of budget processes
  • Implemented a new service model for responding to budget support requests
  • Centralized and tracked help requests to improve responsiveness and expertise matching
  • Created web-based training and information resources to strengthen budget literacy
  • Established a repeatable assessment cycle to measure improvement over time

Integrating Equity-Focused Metrics into Planning and Budgeting

Problem

Traditional planning and budgeting processes did not consistently center equity metrics, limiting the college’s ability to intentionally address disparities in student outcomes.

Outcome

  • Funding allocations more clearly aligned with institutional and statewide equity goals
  • Improved ability to identify institutional practices affecting equitable student outcomes
  • Increased cross-campus ownership and collaboration around equity priorities
  • Stronger alignment between strategic planning, guided pathways, and resource allocation
